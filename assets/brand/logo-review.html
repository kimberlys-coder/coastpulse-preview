Physical Application Check

  • Use the one-color mark for hats, stamps, embossing, and simple screen printing.
  • Drop the gold dot for very small embroidery if it competes with thread weight.
  • Keep the wave and pulse strokes rounded; sharp joins make it feel more medical than coastal.
