How to embed on your website

Copy and paste this code anywhere in your website's HTML:

<iframe src="https://coastpulse.net/widget.html" width="320" height="260" frameborder="0" style="border-radius:12px;"></iframe>

The widget automatically updates whenever the CoastPulse report refreshes (up to 5× daily). No account or API key needed — just paste and go.

Widget reflects SE North Carolina beach conditions: New Hanover, Pender, and Brunswick counties. Data sourced from NOAA National Weather Service, Wilmington office.
