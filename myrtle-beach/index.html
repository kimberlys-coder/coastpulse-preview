Restricted area — dogs generally NOT allowed on the main beach strand May–Sep. Designated pet-friendly areas available off the main beach.
Leash required in all public areas. $1,087 fine for violations in the restricted zone. Check current ordinances before visiting.
✅North Myrtle Beach
Before 9 AM and after 6 PM (Memorial Day – Labor Day); all day rest of year
Leash required. Waste cleanup required. Confirm current rules with the city before visiting.

NOT allowed on the beach May 15 – Sep 15. Allowed rest of year.
Strict enforcement during summer season. Leash required year-round when allowed.

Allowed year-round — no seasonal restrictions

Allowed on beach and in campground year-round
