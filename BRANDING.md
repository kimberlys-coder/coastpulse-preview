# CoastPulse Brand Standards

Daily coastal conditions report — surf, tides, rip current risk, weather, and
shelling/golden-hour extras. Currently previewing Southeastern NC content,
designed to scale to other coastal markets later.

## Colors

| Role | Hex | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| Primary (headings/accents) | `#1B7A8C` | Deep ocean teal |
| Secondary accent | `#F2B85C` | Warm sand/sun gold |
| Body text | `#3A3F44` | Soft charcoal, not pure black |
| Background | `#FAF7F2` | Warm off-white / sand |
| Rip risk — LOW | `#4CAF50` | Green |
| Rip risk — MODERATE | `#F2B85C` | Sand gold (shifted from Sunny's orange) |
| Rip risk — HIGH | `#E0563E` | Warm red |

## Typography

- **Headings:** Poppins (Google Fonts)
- **Body:** Nunito (Google Fonts)
- Fallback: Arial / sans-serif

## Logo

- Primary concept: a rounded coastal wave/horizon with a small sun accent.
- Use the black-and-white version as the master approval test before color.
- Use the standalone mark for favicons, app icons, social avatars, hats,
  stamps, embossing, and other small or one-color applications.
- Keep the logo clear of extra weather icons, location names, shadows, and
  decorative wave layers.
- Files live in `assets/brand/`.

## Voice

- Friendly, informative, beach-community feel — distinct from a radio
  station's on-air voice.
- No references to Sunny 103.7 or any single station/market.

## Naming

- Brand: **CoastPulse**
- Avoid geography-specific terms in product copy (e.g. don't hardcode "NC"
  into headings/components) so regional editions can be added later.
