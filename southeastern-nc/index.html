Shelling & Golden HourTides, beach walks, and evening light
🐚 Shelling conditionsGood

Shell yeah! Good shelling conditions mean the low tide window is worth planning around. Look along the fresh wrack line and just below the last high-tide mark, especially on quieter stretches with less foot traffic. For the best odds, walk slowly within about an hour of low tide and check shell piles near inlets, jetties, and gently sloped sandbars. Today's cue: Low tide at 3:34 PM · WES wind · 1–3 ft surf.

Best timingWithin about an hour before or after low tide.
Where to lookWrack lines, inlet edges, shell piles, and sandbar drop-offs.
Good findsOlive shells, whelk pieces, coquinas, sand dollars, and Scotch bonnet fragments.
🐾 Paws at the BeachLocal ordinances · verify before you go
Are dogs allowed at the beach?
Yes, but rules vary by town. 8 beaches allow dogs during limited hours or seasons; 1 beach has a stricter seasonal ban.
Beach-by-beach dog rules
⚠️Wrightsville Beach
NOT allowed on beach April 1 – September 30. Allowed all day October 1 – March 31.
Leash required at all times. Must wear collar with current rabies tag and town license. Waste cleanup mandatory.
✅Carolina Beach
Before 9 AM and after 5 PM (April 1 – September 30); all day October 1 – March 31.
Leash required at all times. Waste cleanup required.
✅Kure Beach
Before 9 AM and after 5 PM (April 1 – September 30); all day October 1 – March 31.
Leash required (15-ft max). Waste cleanup required.
✅Surf City
Allowed year-round at any hour.
Physical leash required at all times (20-ft max). Off-leash not permitted. Waste cleanup required.
✅Topsail Beach
Allowed year-round. No time restrictions noted in current ordinance.
Leash required (20-ft max). Must clean up after pet.
✅Oak Island
Allowed year-round at any hour. Off-leash permitted October 15 – March 15 only.
Physical leash required year-round except during off-leash season. Off-leash dogs may not enter the surf or water. Waste cleanup mandatory.
✅Holden Beach
Before 9 AM and after 5 PM (May 20 – September 10); all day rest of year.
Leash required. Violations result in fines.
✅Ocean Isle Beach
Before 9 AM and after 6 PM (May – September); all day October – April.
Leash required. Clean up after your pet.
✅Sunset Beach
Before 9 AM and after 6 PM (May – September); all day rest of year.
Leash required. Waste pickup required.
Rules subject to change. Always verify current ordinances with the town before visiting.
