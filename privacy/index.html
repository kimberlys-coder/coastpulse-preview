Privacy Policy

Effective June 19, 2026
Overview

CoastPulse (coastpulse.net) is a free, public beach conditions site. We take your privacy seriously and do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

CoastPulse does not require you to create an account or submit any personal information to view beach conditions.

Our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) automatically logs standard web server data, including IP addresses, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages visited. This data is used solely for security and performance monitoring and is governed by GitHub's privacy policy.

If you subscribe to a CoastPulse newsletter in the future, your email address will be collected and stored by our email service provider (Beehiiv). You may unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email.

Third-Party Services

Google Fonts: We load fonts from fonts.googleapis.com. Google may collect usage data per their privacy policy.

NOAA / NWS: Beach conditions data is fetched from publicly available NOAA and NWS APIs. No personal data is transmitted to these services.

OCEARCH: Marine wildlife data is sourced from OCEARCH's public tracker API.

Advertising: CoastPulse may display advertisements in the future. If implemented, advertising partners may use cookies or tracking technologies. We will update this policy before enabling ads.

Cookies

CoastPulse does not set any first-party cookies. Third-party services (Google Fonts) may set cookies in accordance with their own policies. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Children's Privacy

CoastPulse is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email us at coastpulse@gmail.com.
